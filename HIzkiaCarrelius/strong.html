How to grow stronger in Genshin Impact

Level Up Your Characters:
- Focus on leveling up your characters by gaining experience points (EXP).
- Use EXP materials obtained from defeating enemies, completing quests, or opening chests to level up your characters.
- Ascend your characters to increase their level cap and unlock new abilities.

Enhance Your Weapons:
- Upgrade your weapons to improve their base stats and increase their damage output.
- Use enhancement materials such as weapon EXP crystals and ores to enhance your weapons.
- Consider refining your weapons using duplicate copies to unlock additional effects and increase their power.

Equip Artifacts:
- Equip your characters with artifacts that complement their playstyle and abilities.
- Focus on obtaining artifacts with desirable set bonuses that enhance your character's strengths.
- Level up and enhance your artifacts using artifact EXP materials to maximize their effectiveness.

Unlock and Level Up Talents:
- Invest in unlocking and leveling up your character's talents to unlock powerful abilities and boosts.
- Use talent upgrade materials obtained from elite bosses and domains to enhance your character's talents.

Explore the World:
- Explore the vast world of Teyvat to discover hidden treasures, chests, and resources.
- Complete world quests, commissions, and events to earn valuable rewards and experience.
- Participate in boss fights and domains to obtain rare materials and artifacts for character progression.

Build Synergistic Teams:
- Create teams with characters that complement each other's elemental abilities and reactions.
- Experiment with different team compositions to find synergies that suit your playstyle and combat strategies.
- Adjust your team lineup based on the enemies you encounter and the challenges you face.

Upgrade Your Adventurer Rank:
- Increase your Adventurer Rank by completing quests, exploring the world, and earning Adventure EXP.
- Higher Adventurer Ranks unlock new features, domains, and rewards, allowing you to progress further in the game.

Master Combat Mechanics:
- Learn and master the game's combat mechanics, including dodging, blocking, and elemental reactions.
- Utilize elemental combinations and reactions to deal massive damage to enemies and gain strategic advantages in battle.

Stay Informed and Updated:
- Keep up-to-date with game updates, patch notes, and community discussions to stay informed about new content, events, and strategies.
- Join online communities, forums, and social media groups to share tips, strategies, and experiences with other players.

Have Fun and Be Patient:
- Enjoy the journey of becoming stronger in Genshin Impact and embrace the challenges and discoveries along the way.
- Be patient with your progress and celebrate your achievements as you continue to grow and improve in the game.
