Game System

Gacha System:
Genshin Impact utilizes a gacha system for acquiring characters, weapons, and items.
Players spend in-game currency or real money to obtain randomized rewards from various banners.
The gacha system features different rates for obtaining rare, uncommon, and common items, with pity systems to guarantee higher-rarity drops after a certain number of pulls.
Players can use Primogems, Acquaint Fates, and Intertwined Fates to perform pulls on specific character or weapon banners.

Battle System:
Genshin Impact features real-time action combat with an emphasis on elemental interactions and team synergy.
Players control a party of up to four characters, each with unique elemental abilities and combat roles.
Combat revolves around exploiting elemental reactions such as Overload, Superconduct, and Vaporize to deal damage and control the battlefield.
Dodging, blocking, and timing attacks are essential for success in battles against enemies and bosses.

Event System:
Genshin Impact regularly introduces limited-time events offering special rewards, challenges, and story quests.
Events may include character trials, domain challenges, world exploration events, and co-op challenges.
Completing event tasks and objectives grants event-exclusive currencies and items that can be exchanged for valuable rewards.

RNG Mechanics:
RNG (Random Number Generator) mechanics influence various aspects of gameplay, including loot drops, artifact stats, and gacha pulls.
Players rely on RNG for obtaining rare items, upgrading artifacts, and acquiring powerful characters and weapons through the gacha system.
RNG adds an element of chance and uncertainty to gameplay, requiring players to adapt and strategize based on random outcomes.

Artifact System:
Artifacts are equipment items that enhance character stats and abilities in Genshin Impact.
Players obtain artifacts from defeating bosses, completing domains, and exploring the world.
Artifacts come in sets, with bonuses granted for equipping multiple pieces from the same set.
Upgrading artifacts involves enhancing their stats using artifact fodder and materials obtained from dismantling unwanted artifacts.

Character System:
Genshin Impact features a diverse roster of characters, each with unique abilities, elemental affinities, and combat roles.
Characters are obtained primarily through the gacha system, with some obtainable through story progression, events, and the in-game shop.
Players can level up characters, upgrade their abilities, and equip weapons, artifacts, and accessories to enhance their stats and abilities.

Parkour System:
Genshin Impact features a dynamic parkour system that allows players to traverse the open-world environment with agility and freedom.
Players can climb various surfaces, including walls, cliffs, and structures, by holding the designated climb button.
Certain character abilities and elemental skills may also aid in parkour, such as geo constructs and anemo updrafts that propel characters into the air.

Weapon System:
Weapons in Genshin Impact come in various types, including swords, bows, catalysts, claymores, and polearms, each suited to different playstyles and character roles.
Weapons can be obtained through the gacha system, crafted using materials, purchased from shops, or obtained as quest rewards.
Like characters, weapons can be leveled up, refined, and enhanced using enhancement materials and weapon-specific upgrade materials.

Exploration System:
Genshin Impact features an expansive open-world environment divided into seven regions, each with its own distinct landscapes, biomes, and landmarks.
Players can explore the world freely, discovering hidden treasures, puzzles, and challenges scattered throughout the landscape.
Completing exploration tasks, such as unlocking teleport waypoints and activating statues of the seven, provides valuable rewards and experience points.
